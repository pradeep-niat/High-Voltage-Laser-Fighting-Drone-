# Circuit Description for Drone Laser Prototype

- Connect ESC signal wires to Arduino PWM pins:
  MOTOR1 -> Pin 3
  MOTOR2 -> Pin 5
  MOTOR3 -> Pin 6
  MOTOR4 -> Pin 9

- Laser module controlled via Arduino Pin 10 through a MOSFET or transistor switch.

- Power supply should match motors and laser requirements.

- Use proper ESCs and flight controllers for actual drone control.

- Ensure all grounds are common.
