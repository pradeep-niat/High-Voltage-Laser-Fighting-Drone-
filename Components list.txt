# Components for Basic Laser Fighting Drone Prototype

1. Quadcopter frame kit
2. 4x Brushless motors
3. 4x ESCs (Electronic Speed Controllers)
4. Flight controller board (Pixhawk, KK2, or Arduino-based)
5. LiPo battery (appropriate voltage and capacity)
6. 5V or 12V low power laser diode module (Class 2 or 3R)
7. RC transmitter and receiver or wireless control module
8. Wiring and connectors
9. Propellers
10. Mounting brackets
11. Safety goggles
